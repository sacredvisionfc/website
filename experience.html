Explore Ghana

Your Weekend Adventures

The Central Region is home to Ghana's most iconic landmarks. Weekend excursions take you beyond the pitch — historic castles, rainforest canopy walks, and coastal towns.

Cape Coast Castle
A major historical site with dungeons and the "Door of No Return," detailing the history of the transatlantic slave trade. UNESCO World Heritage site, just 30 minutes from camp.
Kakum National Park
Known for its rainforest canopy walkway, offering a thrilling high-altitude view of nature 40 metres above the forest floor. One of only a handful in Africa.
Fort Amsterdam
A historic Dutch colonial fort in Abandze, Central Region. One of the lesser-known but fascinating heritage sites along the Gold Coast, offering a quieter, immersive historical experience.
Elmina River
A scenic river flowing through the historic town of Elmina. Take a peaceful boat ride, enjoy the views, and experience the beauty of the Central Region's waterways.
Baobab Beach Resort
A beautiful beachfront resort perfect for relaxation and unwinding after a week of training. Enjoy the ocean breeze, swimming, and authentic coastal Ghanaian hospitality.

Safety

Safeguarding & Safety

Player safety comes first. Here's how we look after everyone at camp.

Child Protection Policy
All staff and coaches are vetted and trained in child safeguarding procedures. We operate a strict zero-tolerance policy towards abuse, bullying, and discrimination. Players aged 15–17 are subject to enhanced supervision protocols.
Medical Support
On-site first aid is available throughout the programme. The nearest hospital is within 30 minutes. All campers must provide a medical information form and emergency contact details before arrival.
Insurance & Liability
All participants are required to hold valid travel and medical insurance for the duration of their stay. Sacred Vision provides on-site supervision but participants are responsible for their own insurance coverage.
Emergency Procedures
Documented emergency action plans are in place covering medical emergencies, severe weather, and evacuation protocols. 24/7 staff supervision ensures rapid response to any situation. Parents receive emergency contact numbers upon registration.
Staff Supervision
Dedicated staff provide 24/7 supervision for all residential campers. A designated welfare officer is available for any player to speak to confidentially at any time during the programme.
Parent Communication
Parents and guardians receive regular updates via WhatsApp throughout the programme. A welcome pack with all policies, emergency contacts, and daily schedules is provided before arrival.
