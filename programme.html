Safeguarding

Player Safety & Wellbeing — Built In

Every player at SVFA is supervised, supported, and protected from arrival to departure. We follow GFA-aligned safeguarding standards because parents — local and international — need to know their child is safe.

24/7 Adult Supervision

Trained staff on duty day and night. Designated overnight chaperone in residence at all times.

Background-Checked Coaches

All coaches are GFA-registered, vetted, and trained in player welfare and child protection.

On-Site First Aid & Medical Support

Qualified first-aider on every session. Partner clinic in Winneba for any medical needs.

Secure Accommodation

Gated on-campus housing at Perez University. Visitor sign-in required. Boys and girls in separate blocks.

Daily Parent Updates

WhatsApp group for international parents. Match photos, daily highlights, and direct line to staff.

Confidential Reporting

Players, parents, and staff can report any concern privately to our designated Safeguarding Lead at any time.

Safeguarding Lead: Available 24/7 via WhatsApp +233 24 701 2493 or info@sacredvisionfootballclub.com

Pathway

The Sacred Vision Pathway

Camp isn't the end — it's the beginning. Here's how Sacred Vision develops players beyond the summer programme.

Register & Arrive
Choose your programme (2 weeks, 1 month, or 3 months), confirm your dates, and arrive at camp in Winneba.
→
Train & Compete
6 days/week intensive training — gym, swimming, video analysis, mentorship, and competitive friendly matches against local teams.
→
Talent Evaluation
Formal assessment by coaches and scouts. You receive a written player report covering strengths, development areas, and recommendations.
→
Exposure & Opportunities
Standout players are recommended to partner scouts, agents, and clubs. Ongoing support from Sacred Vision's network for trials and placements.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: March 2026

1. Registration & Booking

Registration is confirmed upon receipt of a 50% deposit. The remaining balance is due before the camper's start date. All payments are processed securely online (mobile money, card, or bank transfer).

2. Cancellation & Refunds

The 50% deposit is non-refundable. The remaining 50% is refundable if cancellation is made more than 21 days before the start date. Cancellations within 21 days of the start date are non-refundable. Sacred Vision reserves the right to cancel any programme due to unforeseen circumstances, in which case a full refund will be issued.

3. Medical & Health

All participants must complete a medical information form before arrival. Participants are responsible for maintaining valid travel and medical insurance for the duration of their stay. Sacred Vision provides on-site first aid but is not responsible for pre-existing medical conditions.

4. Code of Conduct

All participants are expected to behave respectfully toward staff, coaches, fellow campers, and the local community. Sacred Vision reserves the right to dismiss any participant whose behaviour is deemed disruptive, dangerous, or in violation of the academy's values. No refund will be issued in such cases.

5. Liability

Sacred Vision Football Academy takes all reasonable measures to ensure the safety of participants. However, football is a contact sport and injuries may occur. By registering, participants (or their guardians) acknowledge and accept the inherent risks of physical activity.

6. Media Consent

By participating in the programme, campers consent to photographs and video footage being taken for promotional use by Sacred Vision Football Academy, unless written objection is provided at registration.

7. Visa & Travel

International participants are responsible for obtaining the appropriate visa to enter Ghana. Sacred Vision provides an official invitation letter upon receipt of full payment. Sacred Vision is not responsible for visa refusals or travel disruptions.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Ghana.

For questions about these terms, contact info@sacredvisionfootballclub.com.
