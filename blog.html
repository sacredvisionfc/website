Summer Camp 2026 Registration Is Now Open

Sacred Vision Football Academy is officially accepting registrations for Summer 2026. Running from June 15 to August 30, this year's programme brings six days a week of professional coaching, competitive matches in the CRFA Division 2 League, and cultural immersion in Ghana's Central Region. Open to players aged 15–25, with flexible 2-week, 1-month, or full 3-month options. Accommodation, full board meals, gym, swimming, and local transport are all included. Over 200 players developed, 3 signed professionally, and 12+ countries represented, this summer could be your breakthrough.

From Winneba to Professional Football: 3 Players Signed

Since Sacred Vision Football Academy was founded in June 2022, three players have made the leap from our academy in Winneba to professional contracts. What started as a vision to bridge the gap between rural talent and professional opportunity in Ghana's Central Region has become a proven pathway. These signings are proof that talent exists everywhere, it just needs the right environment to develop. Through daily training, competitive Division 2 matches, formal talent evaluation, and exposure to scouts, our player pathway is delivering real results. Over 200 players have been developed through the academy, and this is just the beginning.

Sacred Vision FA 2 – 1 Bright Stars FC

A hard-fought 2-1 victory over Bright Stars FC in CRFA Division 2 showed the character and resilience of this Sacred Vision squad. Winneba Hosts CRFA U-17 Championship on Local AstroTurf

The Central Regional Football Association hosted a two-day U-17 Championship right here at the Enyan Abaasa AstroTurf on July 31 – August 1, 2025. The tournament brought together the region's most promising young talent for competitive group-stage matches, with the championship decider held at 3:00 PM on the final day. Scouts, football administrators, and local fans packed the venue to watch the next generation compete on a quality playing surface. This event highlights the growing football infrastructure in Winneba and the Central Region's commitment to unearthing and nurturing grassroots talent, exactly what Sacred Vision was built to do.

SVFA Partners with Alicante Sports Academy to Expand Player Pathways

Sacred Vision Football Academy is proud to announce our partnership with Alicante Sports Academy, opening a direct pathway for talented players to gain international exposure in Spain. This affiliation strengthens SVFA's mission to bridge the gap between grassroots talent in Ghana and professional opportunities abroad. Through this partnership, standout players from our programme will have the opportunity to train and trial in Europe, adding a new dimension to the player development pathway that has already produced 3 professional signings. Combined with our existing relationship with Frangeo Sunshine Enterprise, SVFA continues to build a network that gives players in Winneba access to the wider football world.

Introducing SVFA Junior, The Next Generation of Sacred Vision

Sacred Vision Football Academy is expanding with the launch of our junior team, @svacademy_jr on Instagram. The junior programme focuses on developing young players in Winneba and surrounding communities, giving them early access to structured coaching, competitive football, and the mentorship environment that defines the Sacred Vision approach. This is a direct investment in the future, identifying and nurturing talent from a younger age so that when players are ready for the senior squad and the Division 2 League, they arrive prepared. Coach Vincent's long-term vision for the academy has always included building from the ground up, and the junior team is a major step in that direction.
