Training Phases
Phase U14 — Foundation 14+ — Performance
Phase 1 Weeks 1–2
Touches & Confidence
  • Ball mastery — dribbling, turns, 1v1
  • Passing — short passes & triangles
  • Fun coordination drills
  • Small-sided games (4v4, 5v5)
  • High-tempo passing patterns
  • First touch under pressure
  • Aerobic fitness (ball-based)
  • High-intensity small-sided games
Phase 2 Weeks 3–4
Awareness & Movement
  • Rondos — 3v1, 4v2
  • Basic formations (2-3-1, 3-2-1)
  • Transition play
  • Conditioned games
  • Formations — 4-3-3, 4-2-3-1
  • Build-up play, pressing & defense
  • Overloads (6v4), transition games
  • Tactical shadow play
Phase 3 Weeks 5–9
Confidence & Decisions
  • 7v7 / 9v9 matches
  • Pressing basics
  • Quick transitions
  • Weekly feedback sessions
  • 11v11 tactical games
  • Set pieces — corners, free kicks
  • Match scenarios under pressure
  • Video & coach analysis
Phase 4 Weeks 10–16
Enjoyment & Exposure
  • Mini tournaments
  • Skill challenges
  • Light position introduction
  • Competitive matches
  • Showcase games — scout attendance
  • Position-specific training
