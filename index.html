About

More Than a Football Camp

Sacred Vision is a GFA-registered club in the Central Region Division 2 League. We built a summer programme around it — open to local and international players who want to train with a real squad, eat local, and experience Ghana the way no European or American camp can offer.

6 Days Per Week Training
Intensive coaching six days a week. Technical drills, tactical sessions, and competitive small-sided games every day.
Gym & Strength Training
Dedicated gym sessions to build power, speed, and athletic performance. Structured programmes tailored to each player's development.
Swimming & Recovery
Pool-based recovery sessions and swimming to aid conditioning, injury prevention, and active rest between training days.
Video & Tactical Analysis
Afternoon sessions breaking down footage and positional play — the same methods used by professional academies.
Professional Mentorship
One-on-one mentorship sessions with professional players. Guidance on career pathways, mindset, and what it takes to go pro.
Friendly Matches & Talent Evaluation
Competitive friendly matches against local teams with formal talent evaluation. Scouts and coaches assess players for future opportunities.
Full Board Meals
Three Ghanaian & Continental meals daily. Breakfast: oatmeal, eggs, sausage, tea. Lunch: jollof rice, fried rice, palava sauce. Dinner: banku with tilapia, yam, rice ball with groundnut soup and more.
Accommodation
On-campus accommodation at Perez University, Winneba. Standard rooms with a ceiling fan, TV, study table, and private washroom.
Local Transportation
Daily transport to and from training facilities, gym, pool, and cultural visits — all included in your camp package.
Medical & Safety Support
On-site first aid and health support throughout the programme. Player safety is always our top priority.
Player Assessment Report
Every camper receives a personal written assessment — strengths, development areas, and next steps.
The Ghana Experience
Cultural sightseeing, community visits, local food, and life in Central Region. Cape Coast Castle, Kakum, Fort Amsterdam, Elmina River, and more.

Flexible Programmes
Choose 2 weeks, 1 month, or 3 months. Start any Monday. No rigid start dates — the camp fits your schedule, not the other way around.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: March 2026

1. Registration & Booking

Registration is confirmed upon receipt of a 50% deposit. The remaining balance is due before the camper's start date. All payments are processed securely online (mobile money, card, or bank transfer).

2. Cancellation & Refunds

The 50% deposit is non-refundable. The remaining 50% is refundable if cancellation is made more than 21 days before the start date. Cancellations within 21 days of the start date are non-refundable. Sacred Vision reserves the right to cancel any programme due to unforeseen circumstances, in which case a full refund will be issued.

3. Medical & Health

All participants must complete a medical information form before arrival. Participants are responsible for maintaining valid travel and medical insurance for the duration of their stay. Sacred Vision provides on-site first aid but is not responsible for pre-existing medical conditions.

4. Code of Conduct

All participants are expected to behave respectfully toward staff, coaches, fellow campers, and the local community. Sacred Vision reserves the right to dismiss any participant whose behaviour is deemed disruptive, dangerous, or in violation of the academy's values. No refund will be issued in such cases.

5. Liability

Sacred Vision Football Academy takes all reasonable measures to ensure the safety of participants. However, football is a contact sport and injuries may occur. By registering, participants (or their guardians) acknowledge and accept the inherent risks of physical activity.

6. Media Consent

By participating in the programme, campers consent to photographs and video footage being taken for promotional use by Sacred Vision Football Academy, unless written objection is provided at registration.

7. Visa & Travel

International participants are responsible for obtaining the appropriate visa to enter Ghana. Sacred Vision provides an official invitation letter upon receipt of full payment. Sacred Vision is not responsible for visa refusals or travel disruptions.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Ghana.
